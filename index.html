About Me

Dan Anthony Dorado is a full-time faculty at the U.P. School of Library and Information Studies where he teaches information technology, research methodology, and statistical analysis. Prior to that, he was an Information Specialist at the College of Engineering Library where he was in-charge of the System and Network Administration and The Learning Commons. He recently finished his master’s degree at the Technology Management Center in U.P. Diliman.
